Ogun State: The Gateway State

Ogun State was carved out of the old Western State by the military administration of General Murtala Muhammed and General Olusegun Obasanjo in 1976. Ogun State is known as the Gateway State due to its strategic position and pioneering role in education, medicine, law and divinity.

About MBGS

Most Beauty Girl In Surulere (MBGS) is a beauty pageant with a unique vision. Unlike most pageants, MBGS is positioned as a platform for empowerment, mentorship and leadership development for young women in Surulere and Nigeria at large.

The pageant is a creative and purposeful beauty and talent competition that discovers and nurtures future national personalities, brand ambassadors, business women, movie stars and super models.

Our Mission

The strategy for this maiden edition of MBGS is to empower young women who will become Surulere ambassadors and project a positive image of the community.

The Concept

We are looking for a young Nigerian female who has charisma, attitude and the right presence to become a strong public face. She should represent our sponsors and partners while inspiring continued brand loyalty and positive influence across Africa and the world.
